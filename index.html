About Me

I am currently working as a senior lecturer (Associate Professor equivalent) in the School of Mathematics, Physics and Computing at UniSQ, Australia. My major research interests are in Biometrics, Deep Learning, and AI.

I received my PhD from UNSW and have led projects funded by Defence Science and Technology Group, Australia.
