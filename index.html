About Me

I am currently working as a senior lecturer (Associate Professor equivalent) in the School of Mathematics, Physics and Computing at UniSQ, Australia. My major research interests are in Biometrics, Deep Learning, and AI.

My major research interests are in Biometrics, Deep Learning, AI Security and Privacy.

I have authored a number of papers published in high-ranking journals, e.g., IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (CCF A), Pattern Recognition (Core A*), IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics (impact factor=12.3), etc.

I received my PhD degree in computer science from the University of New South Wales, Australia.

I have managed several research projects and received a total of over $770K AUD in grant funding.
